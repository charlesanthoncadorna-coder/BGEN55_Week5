Week 5 - Evolutionary Force I: Mutation

Discover how genetic mutation creates variation and interacts with selection to shape populations over time

Estimated Time: 4-5 hours

Module Overview

Total Estimated Time: 4-5 hours (Reading, Activities, and Assignment)

This week, we transition from systems that rearrange existing variation to forces that create and remove variation. You will explore how mutation, the ultimate source of genetic diversity, acts as an evolutionary force on its own and in opposition to natural selection.

Learning Objectives

After completing this module, you will be able to:

  • Model the effect of recurrent mutation on allele frequency
  • Explain the concept of mutation-selection balance
  • Calculate equilibrium frequencies under mutation-selection balance
